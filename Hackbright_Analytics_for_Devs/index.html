Exercise: Analyze Event Data

Purchases Event Collection CSV. Use Google Spreadsheet to answer the following questions about the event data.

Questions:

  1. What's the total revenue from all purchases?
  2. What's the total revenue from whistle purchases?
  3. How many unique customers made purchases?
  4. Which customers purchased the most products?
  5. Which customers spent the most money?
  6. What are the most popular products?
